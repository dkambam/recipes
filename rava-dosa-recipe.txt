1 cup semolina
1 cup rice flour
0.5 cup all purpose flour
0.5 teaspoon hing
Some jeera
some finely chopped green chillies
chopped onions

#preparation
mix all thoroughly
add water to a thin consistency

#procedure
heat pan. 
sprinkle some chopped onions on pan
srinkle mix from above on pan thinly.

Spray some oil.
let it roast till the edges become brown.

Take it off the pan. 

Eat and Enjoy! :)
